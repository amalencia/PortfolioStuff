About Me

My name is Aly! I am both an engineer and a game developer. I have about 6 years of experience working as an engineer and project manager for Naval Reactors - the government organization responsible for administering the Naval Nuclear Propulsion Program. During my time there, I worked with nuclear I&C systems, nuclear analysis, shock qualification, and electrical issues for both aircraft carriers and submarines. After leaving the Navy, I took time off to pursue my dream of learning how to develop video games, learning how to create pixel art, 3D art, and use Unity Game Engine. I am open to both engineering and software development work!

Outside of my career, I enjoy spending time with my dog, playing and developing video games, cosplaying, running, and advocating for LGBTQ+ rights.

Thank you for stopping by! Have a wonderful day!
